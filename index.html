Specifications

Add "ay" to words begining with a vowel

  • Example: apple
  • Outcome: appleay

Words beginning with one or more consonants, move all of the first consecutive consonants to the end, and add "ay".

  • Example: thread
  • Outcome: eadtheay

Move all of the first consecutive consonants to the end, and add "ay". If the first consonants include "qu", move the "u" along with the "q".

  • Example: squirt
  • Outcome: irtsquay

For words begining with "y", treat "y" as a consonant and move the consonant to the end

  • Example: Yawn
  • Outcome: awyay
